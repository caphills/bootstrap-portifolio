About me

Joy grew up in Tanzania, East Africa. Her background is in Business Administration, majored in Marketing. Joy attended College of Business Education, Dar es Salaam. She also attended Glendale Community College in Arizona before moving to Minnesota over 10 years ago. Joy has over 9 years of healthcare experience working with Orthotics and Prosthetics professionals. She currently works as a Revenue Cycle Specialist for the OandP practitioners. She has intensive knowledge of Medicare coding, compliance and reimbursement specifically for Orthotics Prosthetics.

Joy is excited to join the world of everything web! Every evening and most of the weekend she spends learning and practicing with the new tools (HTML, CSS and and soon JavaScript). Joy is determined to put all her time and effort into learning this fast growing field so that she will someday build professionally looking website. She has to have her morning cup of coffee everyday and on the weekend she enjoys spending time with her friends and/or catching a "good" action movie!
